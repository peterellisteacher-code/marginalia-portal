The drafting scaffold

Five sections, one for each move the assessment expects of you. Fill in your work-in-progress here, watch the word count climb toward 800, and export to plain Markdown when you're ready to draft the actual piece.

Criterion codes — what they mean Each section is labelled with a criterion code from the SACE marking rubric. KU = Knowledge & Understanding (you know the philosophical issue and positions on it). R = Reasoning (your arguments are logical and evidence-based). CA = Critical Analysis (you examine strengths and weaknesses, not just describe). C = Communication (clear expression, accurate terminology, acknowledged sources). The numeral (e.g. KU1, KU2) distinguishes sub-criteria within the same strand — sub-criterion 1 is typically the foundational move; sub-criterion 2 builds on it.
