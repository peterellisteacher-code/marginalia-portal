What question keeps you up at night?

The Issues Study is the only assessment in your Stage 1 Philosophy course that asks you to choose your own philosophical question — and then defend your own answer.

This site is a workspace. You'll find an exhaustive bank of philosophical questions across ethics, epistemology, and metaphysics; a Socratic chamber where an AI interlocutor will press you to refine your thinking; a library of readings drawn from the Western tradition; and a drafting scaffold for when you're ready to write.
